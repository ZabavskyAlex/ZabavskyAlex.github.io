FUNNY IMAGINATION

Developing game

Play, decide, learn. Developmental game for children 6-8 years. The battle begins, the player first chooses the spell. The player moves to the task screen (15 task types). In case of successful decision - the spell is applied, otherwise - the transition to the monster.
